Custom Orders and Special Requests

Celebrate in style with a cake or baked treat made just for you. We’ll work closely with you to bring your vision to life, whether it’s a one‑of‑a‑kind cake or specialty pastries designed to make the occasion truly memorable.

Custom Cake Services

We help you design a one‑of‑a‑kind cake that makes your celebration stand out. From weddings and birthdays to anniversaries and corporate events, our bakers work with you to create a centerpiece that’s both beautiful and unforgettable.

What We Offer

Wedding Cakes: Elegant multi‑tiered designs made to match your style, with your choice of flavors, fillings, and decorations. We provide tastings and consultations to make sure your wedding cake is exactly what you envisioned.

Birthday Cakes: From classic and elegant styles to fun, themed designs, we make cakes for every age. Popular choices include superheroes, princesses, sports, and many more.

Corporate Events: From classic and elegant styles to fun, themed designs, we make cakes for every age. Popular choices include superheroes, princesses, sports, and many more.

Special Occasions: Celebrate life’s milestones with cakes made for anniversaries, graduations, baby showers, and any event that deserves something truly special.

Pricing and Lead Time

Minimum Notice: We need at least 48 hours for custom orders. For wedding cakes or big designs, it’s best to order 2–4 weeks ahead.

Pricing: Custom cakes start at $50. The price changes based on size, design, and details. Contact us for your exact quote.

Consultations: Custom cakes start at $50. The price changes based on size, design, and details. Contact us for your exact quote.

Bulk Orders

Planning a large event? We offer bulk ordering for:

  • Cookies and brownies (minimum 2 dozen)
  • Cupcakes (minimum 1 dozen)
  • Pastries and croissants (minimum 1 dozen)
  • Bread loaves (minimum 5 loaves)
  • Custom dessert platters

Bulk orders receive a 10% discount on orders over $100. Contact us for availability and pricing.
