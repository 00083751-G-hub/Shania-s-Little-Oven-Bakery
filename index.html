Welcome to Our Bakery

At Shania's Little Oven Bakery, each bread, pastry, and cake is crafted to reflect care and tradition. We take pride in offering the community freshly prepared, handmade baked goods created with quality ingredients. From our well-loved sourdough loaves to personalized cakes for special occasions, every item is baked daily with commitment and creativity,ensuring that customers enjoy both flavor and genuineness.

Whether you need fresh bread for everyday meals, a delightful desert to mark a celebration, or personalized baked goods for a special event, Shania's Little Oven Bakery is dedicated to serving you with care and quality. We welcome you to be part of our community of happy customers and enjoy the unique touch that handcrafted baking brings.

Site Usage Rules and Policies

To provide every customer with the highest level of service, we kindly ask you to observe the following guidelines:

  • Custom Orders: Please place all custom cake and bulk orders at least 48 hours in advance. Rush requests may be accepted with an added fee.
  • Member Benefits: Registered members enjoy exclusive discounts, priority access to seasonal items, and special birthday rewards.
  • Privacy: Your information is kept secure and never shared with outside parties. Emails are used only for order confirmations and promotional updates you choose to receive.
  • Pickup and Delivery: Orders can be collected at our bakery, with delivery available for qualifying purchases within our service area.
  • Allergen Information: Our products may include common allergens such as wheat, eggs, dairy, and nuts. Contact us for detailed allergen guidance.
  • Cancellations: Custom orders may be cancelled up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund.
  • Quality Guarantee: We are committed to the quality of our baked goods. If you are not satisfied, please reach out within 24 hours of pickup.
